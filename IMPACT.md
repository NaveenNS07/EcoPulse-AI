# Environmental Impact Analysis (IMPACT.md)

## Problem Statement Alignment

EcoPulse directly engages users with their daily activities and the related greenhouse gas estimates to address the core problem: Carbon Footprint Ignorance.

### Feature Alignment Matrix

| Feature | Carbon Problem Solved | User Benefit | Environmental Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Activity Logger** | Invisible accumulation of emissions from daily transit, food, and energy. | Users quantify their lifestyle choices. | Awareness creates behavioral shifts, potentially saving ~800kg CO₂e/yr. |
| **Reduction Simulator** | Users don't know *which* changes make the biggest difference. | Instant visual feedback on high-value lifestyle changes. | Promotes focused, high-impact changes (e.g. dietary shifts reducing footprint by 20-30%). |
| **AI Sustainability Coach** | Generic advice is ignored. Users are unsure what to prioritize. | Personalized, tailored insights based on logged behavior. | Facilitates targeted, actionable daily changes to reduce consumption. |
| **Eco Challenges** | Sustainability is often isolated and boring. | Gamification creates sticky, long-term habits. | Minor recurring adjustments (like "Meatless Mondays" or "Unplugging") build long term reduction baselines. | 
| **Community Impact** | Feeling that individual actions don't matter against global issues. | Social awareness and group achievement metrics. | Normalizes climate positive action amongst local peers, causing cascading adoption. |

## Measurable Outcomes
The project calculates an explicit baseline and current emissions rate allowing users to target an achievable `500kg CO₂e` monthly goal (global standard recommendation paths toward 2.0C limit goals).
